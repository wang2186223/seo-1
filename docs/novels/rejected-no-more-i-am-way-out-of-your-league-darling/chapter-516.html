Observing them, the secretary believed in love again.

Once back, Cedric only relaxed after he sat watching Daniela for some time.

Daniela had felt unusually tired these past days, and upon reaching the office, she immediately took to the sofa for a sleep.

Cedric stole glances at her repeatedly before he finally diverted his attention to his phone.

He had received many voice messages from Rex.

Choosing not to use headphones this time, Cedric converted the audio messages into text to read them.

Cedric was confused.Had they become that close?

Cedric didn't beat around the bush. "When can I meet your boss?"

Rex turned from the now empty stairwell to where Daniela lay asleep on the sofa.

He quietly said to Cedric, "Cedric, why is your wife still sleeping at this hour?"

They came and went, and she remained undisturbed.

Rex wondered if his boss had married a lazy woman.

She was undeniably attractive, though her apparent lethargy was less appealig.

It had to be his imagination.
