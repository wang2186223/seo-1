Daniela lowered her gaze, listening to the steady downpour outside.

Daniela wasn't about to reveal that she led the intelligence department. She knew Olisvine like the back of her hand-Alexander's basement included.

Joyce hesitated. She considered using it as leverage. "I'll give it to you, but can you give me some money as a reward?"

Joyce shot Daniela a wary glance, suddenly feeling uneasy.

Daniela shut her down instantly. "No."

Joyce gawked at her. "If you're asking for a favor,the least you can do is be polite! Do you even want the note? It might be linked to whoever planned the explosion-your enemy, for all you know! This could be a major clue."
