NovelVibe

Chapter 130

Daniela was on her way downstairs after lunch break when the abrupt buzz of her phone halted her mid-step.

She mentioned the interruption to Lillian before stepping outside to answer the call.

As she greeted the caller with a crisp "Hello," a series of footsteps resounded behind her.

When she turned and saw Alexander approaching,the pieces clicked intoplace instantly.

"You've really outdone yourself. A whole new phone number just to reach me before the bidding? Truly impressive."

Alexander caught the icy edge in Daniela's eyes as she scrutinized him.

With the bidding meeting looming, he felt a surge of urgency. "Let me explain, Daniela."

Daniela's calm demeanor remained intact as she offered a small nod. "Fine, go ahead."

In Alexander's memories, Daniela was always the sensitive young woman who felt neglected by his inattention.

However, the woman standing before him now was a stark contrast to his memories. Clad in a sharp business suit, her hair slicked back into a neat bun,she radiated the authority of a seasoned CEO.

Her gaze was cool, detached, as she awaited his forthcoming stumble of words.

In that moment, as Alexander faced her, a wave of discomfort washed over him.

Daniela, once familiar and close, now seemed like a stranger, her distance palpable and chilling.

"Weren't you going to explain?" Daniela pressed on,her voice tinged with casual indifference.

Her curiosity was piqued; she was eager to uncover the kind of elaborate excuses Alexander might unveil.

Just as anticipated, the words started flowing from him.

"Daniela, I realize my visit today might unsettle you,but I can't stand the thought of becoming just another insignificant nobody. Moreover, I believe you're not merely an echo of your father-you possess your own vision, your own strengths, your own dignity. How could you possibly be content with your future partner relegated to a mere househusband? Come on, you're underestimating me and yourself."

His words flowed with a passionate intensity, nearly swaying Daniela's initial skepticism.

Seeing her stunned expression, Alexander felt a surge of hope and continued, "Daniela, I understand you. I know you better than you know yourself. You're not someone who craves power. You may appear strong and decisive now, but I know deep down, you still long to be a little girl."

His statement was capable of thawing the resolve of any woman, regardless of age.

Internally, Daniela couldn't help but admire the performance. Incredible! What a display of eloquence!

Alexander continued, "That's precisely why I'm here today. I'm asking for a chance to stand by your side.I envision a future where, when people look at us, they won't dismiss me as unworthy. Instead, they'll see us as a perfect couple, affirming that your choice was impeccable!"

With a definitive nod, he concluded, "Trust me,Daniela. Let me take the lead on the project.promise, I'll execute it brilliantly!"

His eyes shone with a mixture of hope and determination, waiting eagerly for her response.

Daniela found his silliness almost too much to bear.Yet, since he seemed determined to underestimate her,she felt compelled to play along.

Her lips twisted into a wry smile as she met Alexander's hopeful gaze and replied with a note of sorrow, "Is that really what you believe? If that's the case, it's clear we're not meant to be."

"What?" Alexander's voice cracked slightly as he blinked at Daniela, his expression a mix of shock and confusion. "What did you just say?"

"What I desire is worlds apart from what you pursue.I yearn for someone who values kindness,thoughtfulness, and puts family above the pursuit of riches. When people look at us, they shouldn't see us as the 'perfect couple' Instead, they should be saying, 'Wow! She really knows how to manage him!'"

Stunned and unsure, Alexander could only stand there, struggling to process.

He had meticulously followed his psychology instructor's guidance, so why was it failing him now?

Everything seemed off.

"Moreover, I'm already financially secure. What use do I have for someone who's just chasing more wealth? There's no charm in that." "But..." Alexander's voice faltered, his hands clenching at his sides. "Weren't you always in love with me? How can you just change like that? Daniela,this must be some kind of joke,right?"

Daniela gave a small, sad nod. "I did love you, but since I realized they were unrequited, I forced myself to move on."

Alexander's mind reeled. No! The way things were before was perfect!

"So, you're saying you chose wealth over what we had?" she asked, her voice laced with a mix of disbelief and sorrow.

Just as Alexander opened his mouth to protest,Daniela, her face crumpling with anguish, raised her hand to stop him. "No, please don't. I think I already know your answer."

At that moment, a storm of regret and despair swirled within Alexander.

For the first time, he felt utterly defeated. He had failed Daniela not just once, but twice now.

Watching her walk away, her shoulders slumped and her steps heavy, Alexander was consumed with self-reproach.

He wanted to slap himself until his face stung for being so damn clueless.
