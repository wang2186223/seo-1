For the first time, a cold, consuming fear gripped him -he'd almost lost a fortune that wasn't even his,but entrusted to him by Daniela.

Panic rising in his throat, he rushed into Daniela's office,complexion drained of color.

If he were back at home, Hamilton's look of scorn would be unbearable-he could already picture that icy,disappointed gaze.

Self-loathing churned inside him, dark enough that a part of him wished he could simply vanish rather than face the fallout.

"Let me see what's gone wrong." Daniela barely glanced up from her work, her tone impossibly calm."Sit. Give me a minute. We'll find out what's broken and see how to fix it."

Her collected composure caught Nikolas off guard.Carol, quietly attentive, passed him a glass of water.

His hands shook as he accepted it, white-knuckled and tense.

Carol gave him a reassuring smile. "There's really no need to panic. Isn't this just how business goes?"

Nikolas let out a weary breath. "This is a lesson from my father-my mistake to fix. I missed something important and left an opening he could exploit."

Carol, completely unfazed by the tension, brushed his concern aside. "You're not superhuman, you know.You've been working yourself to the bone.I heard you've barely slept. Look at those dark circles."

But Nikolas was in no mood to dwell on exhaustion.His attention remained locked on Daniela,searching her face for any sign of hope.

He never would have imagined, not in a million years,that Daniela would become his last hope, the person he was desperate to lean on

Life had a twisted sense of humor.

After a thorough review of the situation, Daniela summoned a few trusted aides to join her for a quick strategy session. Once they'd hashed out the details,she turned to Nikolas and laid out a plan. "Your father knows you inside and out. That's why he knows where to strike. Give this method a shot-if it fails, come straight back to me and we'll figure out the next step together."

Without sparing another glance, Nikolas strode away,leaving Kohen standing there-frozen and rattled,unable to pull himself together for a long while.
