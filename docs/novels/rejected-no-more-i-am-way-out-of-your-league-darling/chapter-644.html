A twinge of sadness pierced Daniela's heart as she read through the comments, yet she harbored no resentment.

Children are a twist of destiny; if they are meant to be in your life, they will find a way, and if not, no amount of pushing can change that.

Jack, caught in the middle, threw a glance in Joyce's direction. He couldn't tell which of the two women was Daniela.

Joyce,however,was lost in her own world,addressing the crowd of reporters with a spirited flair. "The vast fortunes of Elite Lux were clearly meant for Jack.Honestly, who else would be the rightful heir? Should the wealth come our way,we'll accept it gratefully. If not,well,it'll find its way to us eventually. There's absolutely no rush."

Joyce's voice was rich with pride, her smile unwavering as she spoke.

Oblivious to the approaching figures of Daniela and Cade, and ignoring Jack's silent pleas for intervention,she continued unabated.
