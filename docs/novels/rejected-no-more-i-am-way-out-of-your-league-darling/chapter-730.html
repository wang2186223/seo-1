Chapter 726

Silence lingered on the other side of the phone as Hamilton processed Daniela's words.

A soft chuckle escaped him eventually. "To think the founder of Elite Lux, once deemed the wealthiest globally, could still impress me-remarkable indeed."

Understanding often required no explanation; the wise grasped it quickly.

Daniela was ready to show her cards.

She trusted that Hamilton would not harm Cedric.

Yet, Hamilton could not assure that his children were safe from Daniela.

Daniela, a fearsome mercenary leader, had nothing to do with his sons. If she chose to kill them all, he could do nothing about it. Holding the phone, Daniela continued, "Mr. McCoy,it's time the younger ones took over. The future belongs to them."

Hamilton had the call on speaker. The room's occupants stared at him, astonished by the boldness of the caller.

Hamilton had led McCoy Group for decades. It was time for him to retire. But relinquishing power was not easy for someone who had wielded it so long.

Daniela's words echoed the silent thoughts of many in the group.

Curiosity surged as they wondered who could embarrass Hamilton so much.

"You're brave. The last one who dared talk like this no longer breathes," Hamilton responded with narrowed eyes and a serene demeanor.

Daniela's reply was colder. "Go ahead and try.Fortune often favors me, turning the odds in my favor." Hamilton paused, then laughed heartily. "Alright! Ms.Harper, you never disappoint. I have only one request. They can fight and compete however they want, but just don't let it end in death."

Daniela had no current plans to harm Hamilton's children.

Before ending the call, Hamilton chuckled. "I eagerly anticipate our meeting, Ms. Harper."

Nikolas had assumed that Daniela's boldness would distance Hamilton and lead to disciplinary actions.

When he tried to reach Hamilton, there was no response.

Out of options, he called Hamilton's ever-present assistant.

The assistant's voice was tinged with concern,remembering Daniela's stern words. "This time, it's serious. Your father won't intervene. You're on your own."

Nikolas was stunned. "So, my father will just stand by and watch us battle it out?"

Nikolas had always interpreted Hamilton's silence as passive approval of his inheritance.

He was taken aback by the sudden change in strategy.

The assistant suggested, "Your father holds Ms.Harper in high regard. If resistance proves futile, why not join forces with her? It's better to have the wise as allies rather than enemies, wouldn't you agree?"

As the conversation ended, a thoughtful expression crossed Nikolas' face.

Could he really consider friendship with Daniela?

Her goals seemed boundless!

The idea of aligning with her felt impossible.

From Nikolas' perspective, Daniela wasn't just after the culprit; she was also pursuing justice for Cedric's bitter childhood. What other reason could there be for her interest in the McCoy wealth? Despite no longer being the wealthiest in the world,Daniela was hardly in need of funds.

Her cooperation with Charles stemmed from her loyalty to Cedric, not from any desire for financial gain.

Nikolas narrowed his eyes thoughtfully. Given Daniela's dedication to Cedric, he decided to take action against Cedric himself.

To Nikolas, Daniela was merely a renowned mercenary. As the eldest heir to a vast fortune, he felt destined to prevail. What was there to fear?

It was Daniela who should be worried!

As Cedric came back, Nikolas quietly intercepted him along the path.

Observing Cedric stepping out of the car with a grave demeanor, Nikolas noted his duplicity.

Cedric was always gentle around Daniela. With others, however, his face was as impassive and cold as marble. "Cedric, let's talk." Nikolas drew near and addressed Cedric politely.

"There's nothing between us that merits discussion,"Cedric responded with a cool lift of his eyelids.

"If not anything else, perhaps we could discuss Daniela?" Nikolas proposed.

As Cedric's driver pulled away, the two made their way to a nearby pavilion.

"You're aware Danielais backing Charles now, right?"Nikolas broached the subject.

Cedric's expression remained unaffected.

"Do you aspire to rise to power yourself, Cedric?"Nikolas probed further.

Casting a measured glance at Nikolas, Cedric understood that such generosity would not be offered without a motive.

The sole reason Nikolas would approach him was out of a sense of threat. Without a doubt, that threat had come from Daniela.

"And you want me to join your side?" Cedric had made it clear he had no interest in the McCoy fortune, recognizing Nikolas' approach as both a test and an attempt at alliance. "You want me to deal with my wife?"
