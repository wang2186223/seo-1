This wasn't just money. It was open access.

"I won't let her lose a single cent," he said, chest tight with resolve.

Nikolas turned and left without another word, his steps heavier with purpose.

Behind him, Carol exhaled quietly.

Daniela didn't hand out favors. She never gave money, let alone opened every door within her companyfor someone else's business.

So,who was this really for? It wasn't about Nikolas.
