That was exactly how it went down, though Caiden would never admit it.

But Daniela's words always had a way of slicing to the core, and he couldn't see why. This wasn't the same sweet, wide-eyed little girl he once knew.

"Daniela, how can you be so cynical about your father?" Katrina interjected hastily, her voice dripping with insincerity. "You're his blood. He's regretted severing ties ever since. Regardless of your financial status, he would've welcomed you back."

After all, this was merely a beta test-the more user feedback, the better.

Free advertising? Why not? Daniela nodded again.

Later, as Katrina descended for a drink, she was met by the surreal sight of Joyce kneeling before Daniela.

Katrina had devoted her life to boosting Joyce's status, yet here was her daughter, discarding it all to grove before Daniela.
