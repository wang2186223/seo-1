Go fuck Archy somewhere else."

Willow is a good Luna, but that's walking drama. No one will want them but these days GreenMoon applicants get turned down because of his reputation. That's what happens when you get shitty Alphas what we do reflects on them, just as what they do reflects on me.

"They're *alive*, but he'll never walk right again. Who do you think he's going to blame the rest of his immortal life?" I asked.

"Me sir." He said firmly, like a man to another man. "The reputation of GreenMoon is consistently tarnished by *me*.. I can get mad at Reeta, Gloria, or Tina all I want. But I'm Alpha."

"Yes. And *you're* responsible for the medical bills and surgeries *I had to pay for.* A cool 200k. Had you not made them leave with your cruelty, they would have Never been out there." They wouldn't have stolen my car either, but that's petty, so I won't mention it. I have a feeling insurance won't pay up either but to be fair that damn car had NO room for pups. "She praised your pack management, even holding scars from such soft skin being lashed."

He used an Alpha's whip on an Omega. Not illegal, but I did shoot Tyson a text.

321.And Pissed Off King.

He appeared directly behind him, snorting fire, smelling of brimstone. I held my laugh. To me, his screams were hilarious, just deserts for being incompetent, petty, and childish.

"What The Fuck is he doing to Omegas?" Hissed Tyson. His soft spot for the weak might get Alpha Green Killed.

"You saw." I motioned him. "Explain to your king why you beat them, and why you let your mark mate haze your true So harshlyJust a Hair from breaking the law."

*now he was scared.*

"Be honest. We both hear your buzzing." I said with a glare. He was going to blame Gloria too it's what he'd probably told the pack since he started losing his temper.

And packs follow their Alpha's lead, so the pack likely blames her too, even if they know better. That's what they'll say, since it's the acceptable explanation. Most times, they agree with us, even when they don't because this isn't a democracy.

There isn't a vote.

There Isn't a way to challenge unless they're ready to take it outside so it's safer to nod, bow, and roll over.

".I was Pissed Ok! Is that what you want to hear?!" He asked, and Tyson slapped him. He slapped him hard enough to knock him right on his ass. A taste stronger and he'd probably be passed out.

"I want to hear the Truth." He hissed.

"And nothing could compare." I said in understanding The bond break hurts mentally and physically. We left him alone a moment.

Let him grieve.

Rejecting your mate, in my opinion, is almost like fucking cursing yourself There is Only One. And to deny it, is to be eternally wanting for it as an immortal. A thousand years can pass, and he will still feel like this was yesterdayforever.

He will never see his mate's smile He will never know his pups unless he comes to visit, which I doubt since it's so awkward. He will never know how nice it is just to wake up, and she's right there with the kids, and it's your turn to take them to the toilet, bathe them, and brush their teeth.

He will never know what it feels like for the mundane to feel as magic never see his pups grow to be men, to be with them their first kill.

Some of my closest, fondest memories his children won't get. Bastards in our culture don't happen often so it is looked Down on harshly. Those women will be judged and shunned as harlot pack breakers. Alpha ruiners, when it's Him who's the Problem.

I should gut his ass right now. Flay 'em while he's still breathing. It would be easy, much easier to be a monster than a prince any day.

Oh, and she'll probably fit on the playpen floor. I finally got to see why Reeta fled for her life. That Dirty Bitch beat her within an inch of her life, knowing she was with pup.

What the fuck could Reeta do? She has no power and what power she was supposed to have, Green didn't provide... He didn't know details, but I know he felt that

A white scroll appeared in my hands with the Moon on it.

I burned it.

I have been barred from flaying them. Tyson too He has no idea how his goddess has saved his worthless pelt. I've been wanting a floor rug for the office, and he's a light enough shade to dye cobalt blue.

Alpha Green realized all that now. His wolf took over, and cried bitter tears on his knees. Gabble, I felt sorry for. The wolf isn't in control, merely a passengerHe had to watch this play out.

It makes me thankful there is no human side to me This is a lot of power and authority to hold, too much to be corrupt.

\[It is all his fault.\] Gabble whined, losing his form, laying on his side as if he'd been stabbed in the heart. The bond thread snappedShe'd finally rejected him back. Alpha Green's wolf was so hurt as he lay there But he did this.

"Nobody made him reject his mate. He did that part all on his own." Said Tyson. In the eyes of the Wolf King, he deserved no pity. "Nobody made him get Gloria pregnant, or make Tyna Luna when she Shouldn't have been. Nobody told him to reject and badmouth his mate because she was an Omega. And YOU allowed Tyna to punish her for Nothing. You Knew she was "

I need to find Reeta now that I think of it. She's an Omega, so her wolf's dormant but I know this still hurts. The bond break affects them both. Green's wolf still didn't give him back control His instinct was to go make this right somehow.

But it's too late for that. The bond's snapped that's It.

That was done by grandmother.

"That you break my grandmother's heart yet she still protects you lot." I growled. I don't know if that was even proper English. I don't care. I wanted to punch him I can't. I can't hold myself back right now.

If they got together now, they'd basically be mark mates.

\[I told him to take her that the Goddess does not make mistakesI told him now we have *nothing* like I saidJust like I said.\] Gabble screamed on his side on the floor like Like a Dog? It kind of took away from how fucked up this was.

/. Why does he sound like a Husky?/ I linked. Like a WeTube Short like huskies when they don't have their way.

\[This is my fault!! \] He wailed Alpha Green did it again. I Was right, but his pack members thought we were monsters because he were chuckling.

/Yeah! Remember the video grandpa Kaiden sent? When Bagel had the temper-tantrum?/

She laid in the tub and complained because it wasn't bathtime yet.

I made Tyson chuckle.

/I hear it too. Wa-Wa-Wa/ He linked.

/You're a dick./ I linked back with a grin. /Wa-wa-wa is gonna be a thing now. I'm doing it./

/He did this to himself. Boo-fuckin'-hoo./ He rolled his eyes. Tyson outright laughed. /Wa-wa-wa./

Grandfather left. He's a god who holds a mate, one that was imprisoned. It was a little much for the Horned One, and that's saying something.

He will never know the touch of his mate ever again. Something I cannot risk with Willow.
