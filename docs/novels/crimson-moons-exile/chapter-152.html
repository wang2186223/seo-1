Reeta was safe, and she was with pup. About two months along according to grandfather He also blessed that pup because grandma asked. He will be a son, one who will be about as strong as Gilbert. So strong enough to replace a dead council member. I also already texted Tyson, who's elated.

Green has agreed to just pay out lump sums for his pups, 3 million each, and an Omega should be here with those checks this afternoon.

It will be refused.

He will pay *each* month. I won't let him 'forget' about his pups. If this happens again, I'm going to get him neutered. That man's probably making Arviel jealous.

I chuckled, waking myself up a bit, while my mate and Eternya did the rest.

Laughter woke me up I'd have to settle for a shorter nap than I'd planned. I said I'd watch the boys today anyway, but thankfully they snored on my chest.

I also caught the bed ablaze from my damn candle's flicker, however Tyson shared how to repair the area by trading magical energy for matter. As a demigod, I am not allowed to break the laws of reality fully. Like, I can't make something that doesn't exist from *nothing* like grandfather or the Three Princes, however, I *can* exchange my magic for materials.

I think he said the conversion was a soul's worth of energy to repair a house my size.

"What are they doing down there?" I asked with a chuckle. The boys were taking a nap on my chest, but as soon as I woke up, they were ready to go. They followed me with a little bounce, tails in the air following papa, not wondering off to play with the ton of toys left out for them.

After all they are teething. There's still no guarantee I'll wake up to having unburned shoes

We went home so I could protect my pack. There's nothing personal with Tyson anymore, but my mortals hold my priority. Misfit was built for the thrown away, forgotten, lost, and discarded.

Apparently, I'm starting to get the nickname of The Goatherder. If it keeps up, it will become a real title in 20 years or so.

Basically, I'll become stronger when protecting my homewoerld.

Hahaha!"
