"All right, looks like she needs you more anyway,"Arabella replied, her voice cold and steady.

Puzzled,Joyce shot Arabella a suspicious glance before heading straight for Elissa's hospital room.Reaching the door, she was met with unexpected moaning sounds from inside.Confused,she checked the room number again.It was definitely the right one.

A strange feeling crept in. Did Elissa have a boyfriend? But she had never mentioned anything about that before!

Right as Joyce reached for the doorknob, she caught the sound of a familiar voice drifting from inside the room.

Elissa's words rang out. "Tell me, who do you really love more? Me or Joyce?"

"It's always been you, babe," Kenny said, his voice rushed and uneven. "If you hadn't pushed me to stick around Joyce, I wouldn't have wasted a second on her!"

A soft, teasing laugh slipped from Elissa, her tone thick with fake sweetness. "I knew it. Deep down, I'm the one you want most."

The words hit Joyce like a splash of freezing water,leaving her rooted to the spot.

From inside, the sounds only grew more intimate.Kenny's ragged breathing mixed with Elissa's flirtatious moans. A sharp ache formed in Joyce's chest.

Back in her first days at college, Joyce had met Kenny through Elissa.

Kenny's looks had been striking, but Joyce wasn't smitten right away. She always knew her family background meant she could have chosen someone much more impressive,especially now that the Hyde family's influence was fading.

Over time, Kenny's persistence wore her down,and Elissa never stopped talking him up.

Eventually, Joyce let herself fall for him.

For so long, she believed she was truly fortunate, blessed with both a loyal friend and a caring boyfriend. Only now did she see the truth. She had been completely deceived.

None of it was real. Looking back, she should have spotted the warning signs.

Elissa's harshness toward Daisy should have been a red flag, but Joyce kept telling herself Elissa had a good heart.

Rage bubbled up inside Joyce as the truth crashed down on her. She was ready to barge in and expose them both. But just as her fingers brushed the door, someone grabbed her hand and pulled her away.
