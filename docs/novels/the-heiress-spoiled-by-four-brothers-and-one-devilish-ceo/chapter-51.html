She nearly cracked a molar from clenching her teeth.That freaking adopted daughter of the Reids was seriously trying to steal her man?

"Amelia,your brothers Samuel and Jason are on stage too! The Lewis family really is top-tier in Lindon City."

"With powerhouse families like the Reids and Lewises,no wonder they're winning bids left and right. That necklace will be yours, for sure!"

But Amelia knew better. Jason and Samuel would never give her something that expensive-she wasn't their real sister, after all.

Even before they found out the truth, they'd never really been close to her. Now that they knew? Forget it.

She kept her voice soft. "A necklace like that? It's probably going to our mother."

Lenna looked at her, eyes full of admiration."You're the only girl in your family, right? Your brothers must dote on you endlessly."

Jessica sighed with a dreamy look. "Four brothers...Man, I'd settle for just one. Honestly, just give me Jason and I'd be good."

The two kept chatting excitedly about the four Lewis brothers, each exaggerating more than the last.No one noticed the brief flicker of gloom in Amelia's eyes.
